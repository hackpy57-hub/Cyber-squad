About SEC-ROOTS

SEC-ROOTS is a dedicated platform for learning practical ethical hacking. Our mission is to provide high-quality, accessible education on security tools like Kali Linux and Termux. We believe in learning by doing, providing hands-on labs and real-world scenarios.

Whether you are a beginner curious about cyber security or looking to advance your skills, we provide the foundational knowledge and advanced techniques you need to succeed in this field.
